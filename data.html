About the 'outlier'¶

MERVL-int copy chr14:57417435-57419173 have one and only one open reading frame.

Length:1632 (recall length of the three clusters 1671bp, 1710bp, 1732 bp).

Sequence Structure incomplete. ID_B1-MT2B1-MERVL_int-MT2B-ID4. ID: SINE related to retroposition
